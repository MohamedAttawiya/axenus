AXEN LABS Product Lines

Two lines, one principle: precision chemistry engineered for proven results.

The ETHRIX Line is designed for ethanol-based performance applications — enhancing power output, combustion stability, and throttle response across ethanol blends (E10–E85).

ETHRIX Absolu

High-purity ethanol blend enhancer that increases octane stability and optimizes combustion for all ethanol-blended fuels.

Core product for ethanol optimization

ETHRIX Ignis

Track-grade octane accelerator engineered to reduce detonation and enhance throttle response. Designed for high-performance racing and flex-fuel vehicles.

High-performance / racing applications

ETHRIX Flux

Advanced ethanol combustion optimizer that refines fuel atomization and burn rate for improved efficiency and cleaner emissions.

Combustion efficiency & consistency enhancer

The AXION Line protects and preserves — engineered to combat ethanol’s downsides, including corrosion, phase separation, and system wear. Built for long-term stability and reliability.

AXION Shield

Anti-corrosion system protector that defends fuel components from moisture and ethanol-induced wear. Ensures long-term durability and clean fuel delivery.

Routine additive for all E-blends

AXION Vault

Long-term fuel stabilizer that prevents phase separation and oxidation during storage. Ideal for boats, generators, and seasonal vehicles.

Long-term protection & storage stability
