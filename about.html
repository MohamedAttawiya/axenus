Our Story

From refinery floors to precision laboratories — Axen Labs was born from hands-on fuel engineering and two decades of real-world performance experience.

Origins

Axen Labs traces its roots to Gateway Ethanol LLC, where our founders worked as employees advancing ethanol-blend fuel technologies in the early 2000s. When Gateway Ethanol LLC ceased operations in 2009, we refused to let that innovation end there.

Re-established independently as Axen Labs, we continued refining high-purity ethanol and additive chemistry — this time with a sharper focus on efficiency, calibration, and measurable performance.

Experience & Presence

With over 15 years of field-tested expertise, Axen Labs operates both as a research-driven brand and a practical supplier. Our Ohio-based outlet has served regional performance and agricultural markets — particularly ethanol-enhanced fuels for tractors and heavy machinery — with consistency and integrity.

Team

We’re a close-knit team of five engineers and specialists — each committed to advancing fuel chemistry through verifiable data, not speculation. Every formulation, from concept to field test, reflects our shared belief in one principle: Precision. Proven.
