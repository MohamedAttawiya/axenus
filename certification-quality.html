Certification & Quality

Our chemistry is validated against stringent global standards. We operate on measured engineering, traceable batches, and documented compliance — because performance is only meaningful when it’s proven.

Quality Systems

Frameworks that govern how we design, produce, and continuously improve our products.

ISO 9001
Quality Management System — process control, documentation, and continuous improvement.
ISO 14001
Environmental Management System — reduced impact and compliant waste handling.
ISO 45001
Occupational Health & Safety — safe operations for people and facilities.
CARB Approval
California Air Resources Board — emissions compliance for applicable chemistries.

Fuel & Material Standards

Specifications that our ethanol fuels, blends, and compatible materials are engineered to meet.

ASTM D4806
Ethanol Fuel Standard — anhydrous ethanol requirements for blending.
ASTM D5798
Fuel Ethanol (E85) — volatility and composition specification.
ASTM D4814
Gasoline Blending Standard — quality parameters for gasoline/ethanol blends.
SAE J1681
Material Compatibility — guidance on fuels vs. polymers/elastomers/metals.

Regulatory Compliance

Registrations and frameworks for safe handling, labeling, and transport of chemical products.

EPA Registration (40 CFR 79)
U.S. EPA Fuel Additive Registration — chemistry identity and emissions data.
REACH Compliance
EU chemical safety & registration for substances placed on the EU market.
RoHS
Restriction of Hazardous Substances — limits designated substances in products.
GHS SDS
Globally Harmonized System — standardized Safety Data Sheets & labeling.
NFPA 30
Flammable & Combustible Liquids Code — storage and handling practices.
DOT 49 CFR
Hazmat transportation — classification, packaging, and shipping compliance.
