AXION Shield

Fuel System Defender

$32.00 USD

AXION Shield neutralizes the corrosion, moisture, and varnish issues that ethanol can unleash on fuel systems. From classic carbureted rides to the latest DI platforms, Shield keeps components clean and responsive.

  • Long-term rust inhibition for tanks, rails, injectors, and pumps
  • Lubricates seals and o-rings to prevent ethanol drying and leaks
  • Active detergents remove varnish and stabilize idle quality

Why preservation experts rely on Shield

Shield was engineered with marine, powersport, and collector vehicles in mind. Its anti-corrosion complex forms a micro-film that seals against moisture intrusion even in high-humidity environments.

  • Safe for catalytic converters and oxygen sensors
  • Stops sticky throttle bodies caused by ethanol varnish
  • Restores crisp startup after seasonal storage

Ideal applications

Every ethanol-exposed system benefits, but Shield shines in equipment with intermittent use where corrosion typically takes hold first.

  • Boats, PWCs, and small engines
  • Classic cars with modern ethanol exposure
  • Daily drivers that demand long-term reliability

Usage directions

Treat each 15 gallon fill-up with one 8 oz bottle. For storage prep, double the dose and run the engine for five minutes to circulate through injectors and rails.
